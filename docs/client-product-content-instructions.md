# Client Product Content Instructions

## What To Share
- Fill one row per product in `client-product-master-template.csv`.
- Use `client-product-field-guide.csv` to understand each field before filling.
- If a field is unknown, leave it blank instead of guessing.
- Keep naming consistent across all products.

## Media Rules
- `main_image_link`: provide the single best hero image.
- `gallery_image_links`: provide at least 3 to 6 product images.
- `lifestyle_image_links`: strongly recommended for premium presentation.
- `detail_closeup_links`: include stitching, joinery, grain, marble, edges, hardware, and craftsmanship details.
- Use high-resolution files, ideally `2000px+` on the long edge.
- Do not send webpage URLs when direct file URLs are available.

## Writing Rules
- `title`: short, premium, clear.
- `short_description`: compact summary for listing cards.
- `full_description`: explain design intent, comfort, materials, and ideal usage.
- `customization_note`: clearly mention what can change.
- `alt_text_main_image`: describe what is visible, not marketing copy.

## Link Rules
- Prefer direct links to JPG, PNG, WEBP, MP4, PDF, DWG, GLB, or USDZ.
- If using Google Drive or Dropbox, ensure sharing permissions are public or correctly granted.
- Keep filenames identifiable so media can be matched easily to the product.

## Best Workflow For Client
- First fill identity, category, descriptions, and dimensions.
- Then add all image and video links.
- Then add SEO keywords, room tags, style tags, and material tags.
- Finally fill approval status and last updated date.

## Minimum Required Fields To Launch A Product
- `title`
- `collection_name`
- `category`
- `short_description`
- `full_description`
- `customization_note`
- `primary_material`
- `dimensions_overall`
- `main_image_link`
- `gallery_image_links`
- `alt_text_main_image`
- `search_keywords`
- `room_tags`
- `style_tags`
- `material_tags`
